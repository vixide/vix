# Trademarks

Add the trademark symbol ™ to "Vix" and "Vix IDE" at their first occurrence on
major public-facing pages, including `docs/*`, `README.md`, and `index.md`.

At the bottom of each such page, add:

> Vix™ and Vix IDE™ are trademarks.

The root `LICENSE` and `LICENSE-*` files and the license documentation page
(`spec/license`) carry the same marks. The Vix menu's **License** item shows the
license and trademark information in-app.
